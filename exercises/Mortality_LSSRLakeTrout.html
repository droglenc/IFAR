Curtis (1990) examined the population dynamics related to the recovery of an offshore Lake Trout population near Stannard Rock, Lake Superior. As part of this study, mortality rates were estimated from the relative abundance of Lake Trout longer than 43.2 cm. Relative abundance was recorded as the catch-per-unit-effort (CPE) of each age group in each year expressed as the number of fish caught per 50,000 m of 114.3 mm mesh gillnet.
